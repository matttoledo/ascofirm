CORPORATE LEGAL ASSISTANCE


It is not easy for a company to untangle the practical and legal aspects of the business world. In other words, corporate law can be very complicated if not managed by an experienced business professional.
Our corporate lawyers work closely with clients in multiple areas on sensitive issues and national and international transactions.
Whether the client is an entrepreneur, a fast-growing company, a public department or a multinational corporation, our experience allows us to offer detailed and tailored corporate legal advice.
We provide legal and extrajudicial assistance in the following areas of corporate law:


-Incorporation of companies and non-profit entities;
-Acquisition and sale of equity investments and companies;
-Capital transactions and contributions;
-Transformations, mergers and demergers;
-Transfers, leases, transfers of company branches;
-Shareholders' agreements;
-Obligations and responsibilities of directors and statutory auditors;
-Joint ventures and associations in participation;
-Assistance and advice to governance and management bodies.
-Assistance and consultancy in the preparation and execution of Security Policy Document and governance models.


Corporate legal assistance is provided both in ordinary and extraordinary management,so as to ensure the proper functioning of the entire company, and in particular for those critical moments such as bankruptcy and insolvency proceedings, mergers and acquisitions, issuance of bonds, debt securities and financial instruments, capital increases and reductions, judicial administrations, participation in arbitrations.
We offer assistance from incorporation to dissolution of the company,through the vital management of the company, assisting clients to prevent and resolve any corporate litigation.
The firm's client companies can request a legal domiciliation service for Italian and foreign companies.
