BANKING AND FINANCIAL LEGAL ASSISTACNCE


Banking and financial law is in a transitional period in which banks and financial institutions are facing regulatory changes, strict capital requirements, changing risks and technological challenges.
We help companies to address these problems and to understand the most relevant issues in this dynamic and competitive sector. Through in-depth knowledge of our clients and their strategies, we provide practical and effective advice in order to preserve their relationships with banks and protect them from potential risks.
The professional assistance of Ascofirm operates in the following fields of banking and financial law:


-Stipulation and management of banking and financial contracts;
-Transparency relations between bank/customer;
-Management and banking and financial consulting of the company.

ASCOFIRM
Asconfirm is a team of professionals dealing with consulting, management, corporate, accounting, financial and tax assistance to companies. Thanks to its network of professional relationships, Asconfirm operates all over the world: Italy, Europe, Russia and the United States.
