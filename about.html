WELCOME


Asconfirm, founded and directed by Valerio Scimemi, offers consulting and management, legal, corporate, accountig, financial and tax assistance to companies. Our expertise is based on the recognition of the company professional as a necessary figure for all those companies willing to protect their work, interests and rights.
As a firm specializing in corporate law, we act both at the time of the legal dispute and at a previous time, avoiding to end up in litigation thanks to the work done previously and the resolution of issues from the beginning.
We believe it is crucial to establish a strong and lasting bond with your business professional, since such problems are often the result of a lack of knowledge of the law and of the means provided by the law to protect corporate interests.

Our experience in business consulting, supported by our deep knowledge of national and international markets, guarantees the respect of corporate law in all its forms. Business issues, of whatever nature, need a resolution that can meet the need of each cliente: in our offices companies can find the assistance they need, with the security of a team of young, dynamic and experienced professionals. Finally, we believe that there are no standard solutions for all companies. Every company lives a different path and it is essential to provide company assistance that considers every peculiarity. That´s why our team is specialized in all business fields and this knowledge, together with the needs of the client company, provides the ideal path to solve every issue and meet every need of the business.

LOCATIONS

Asconfirm is based in Italy in Rome, Milan, Florence and Palermo and operates, through its network of professional relationships, in Europe in London, Russia in Moscow, United Arab Emirates in Dubai, China in Hong Kong, Shenzhen and Shanghai and the United States in New York.
