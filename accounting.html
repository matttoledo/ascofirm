ACCOUNTING


To keep pace with the rapid and dynamic changes in the competitive environment, companies need to be agile. This means implementing an 'integrated' compliance of administrative activities to translate the heterogeneous regulatory and procedural environment that affects accounting, tax and legal requirements into processes.
Ascofirm provides professional services in terms of administrative and accounting management. We work with national and international clients, with the possibility of multilingual solutions integrated and interactive with the client.
Our services:


-Telematic services
-Temporary administrative-accounting assistance
-Administrative and accounting management
-Administrative personnel management
-Tax representatives for non-Italian residents
-Services in the context of Securitization Transactions and Covered Bond Issuance Programs
-Electronic storage/replacement storage
-Processing tax returns via web platform.

ASCOFIRM
Asconfirm is a team of professionals dealing with consulting, management, corporate, accounting, financial and tax assistance to companies. Thanks to its network of professional relationships, Asconfirm operates all over the world: Italy, Europe, Russia and the United States.
