ASSISTANCE IN NATIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL CONTRACTS


Every company needs to protect itself during its lifetime: the purchase and sale of real estate, the acquisition of a strategic asset and the drafting of any contract can play a decisive role in the natural course of business.
In order to avoid any risk or negative consequences, it is important to act immediately, preventing any problems with appropriate legal assistance.
We provide legal advice, both judicial and extrajudicial, in the following areas of civil law:


-Securities and real estate buying and selling;
-Contracts and mandates;
-Supply of services, administration;
-Assignment of credit without and with recourse;
-General conditions of sale and purchase;
-Lease also for commercial use, rent, loan;
-Rental of vehicles and boats;
-Subordinate, para-subordinate, mediation, agency;
-Leasing, factoring, handling, engineering;
-Insurance policies;
-Recovery of receivables (movable and immovable property enforcement procedures, from third parties, opposition to execution and enforcement deeds, seizures, mortgage registrations, participation in encashments per person to be appointed);
-Actions for various kinds of damages.
