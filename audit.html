AUDIT & ASSURANCE


Ascofirm deals with Audit and Assurance, a service that allows companies to respond to the information needs of the market and investors.
The Assurance and Legal Audit services guarantee more credibility to companies. In fact, they are quality indicators towards shareholders, customers, stakeholders, suppliers, regulators and investors and give a competitive advantage in the capital market (both for commercial agreements and for the company's growth path).
The services offered include:


-Financial Statement Audit
-Assurance Services
-Transaction Services
-Information Risk Management
-Fairness opinions
-Business plan reviews and pro-forma financial information
-Advice and assistance in the listing process
-Transition to IAS/IFRS
-Management Control System Assistance
-Assistance with administrative and accounting procedures
-Financial Reporting Control Systems

ASCOFIRM
Asconfirm is a team of professionals dealing with consulting, management, corporate, accounting, financial and tax assistance to companies. Thanks to its network of professional relationships, Asconfirm operates all over the world: Italy, Europe, Russia and the United States.
